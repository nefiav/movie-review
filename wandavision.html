Wandavision Review
6 February 2021
Wandavision

This show has great elements of action, horror and mystery. Honestly, it was probably a hard job to top Avengers: Endgame, but everyone who worked on this show managed to exceed all expectations.

I would rate this show 5 stars out of 5! I also recommend that everyone watch it right now so they can be part of the mystery.

5 Stars!
